Your Stoker apps run on Google Cloud projects, which are also Firebase projects.

## Firebase Auth Email Templates

You can customize the email templates used for auth actions in the Firebase Console. Settings are under Authentication > Templates.

## Firebase Integrations

There are a number of services that your Firebase (Google Cloud) projects can integrate with. See "Integrations" in the "Project Settings" section of the Firebase Console.

## Project Alerts

Firebase sends alerts regarding your Firebase (Google Cloud) projects. You can enable/disable these under "Alerts" in the "Project Settings" section of the Firebase Console.
